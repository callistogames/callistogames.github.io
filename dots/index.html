How to Play

Move the blue square with your cursor to "eat" the randomly spawning green dots and keep the timer up. At the same time, avoid the red dot. The square will grow with every green dot you eat, making it easier to catch new dots—but harder to avoid the red one! The game is over when you eat a red dot or the timer runs out.
